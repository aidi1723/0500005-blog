## English

**The 0.5 Chronicles** is not a loose archive of essays. It is being built as a volume-based history of how ordinary life crossed from the late mechanical age into the AI era.

The project moves along two lines at once:

1. **Retrospective volumes** that rebuild the transition years from roughly 1980 onward.
2. **Now Records** that preserve the present tense as source material from inside the transition itself.

This roadmap explains how the main historical line is currently structured.

### Volume I — Digital Eve (1980–1995)

Volume I records the years before digital civilization became normal life.

It follows the late mechanical age, early computing habits, toolchains, office systems, training, reliability, and the gradual arrival of networked thinking. By the end of this volume, the internet has entered the center of the age, but it has not yet fully reorganized daily life.

In one sentence:

> **Volume I is about the formation of digital civilization before it became ordinary life.**

### Volume II — The Age of Connection (1996–2010)

Volume II records the years when the internet entered ordinary life.

This is the period of dial-up, inboxes, search, cybercafes, commercial webpages, digital memory, instant messaging, forums, broadband, feeds, folk classification, touchscreens, apps, cloud habits, and the visible stream of everyday life.

In one sentence:

> **Volume II is about the internet becoming ordinary life, then entering the pocket, the cloud, and the stream.**

### Volume III — The Platform Age (2011–2020)

Volume III records the years when platforms began to organize ordinary life from within.

This is the period of mobile payment, messaging ecosystems, behavioral data, recommendation systems, deep learning’s public breakthrough, API composition, voice interfaces, hidden automation, machine perception, and remote coordination at scale.

In one sentence:

> **Volume III is about platforms, mobile systems, data, and algorithms becoming the everyday machinery of social continuity.**

### What comes next

The likely next historical line is a fourth volume covering the years after 2020.

That volume would likely follow the acceleration of remote-first habits, foundation models, generative AI, agent-like workflows, and the growing shift from platform society toward machine-mediated cognition and labor.

The exact title and final scope remain open, but the direction is already visible.

### Why the project is called “0.5”

The phrase **“0.5”** names a transitional civilization.

It describes a world that is no longer fully mechanical, but not yet fully AI-native; a world in which humans, institutions, software, and infrastructures are still crossing an unfinished threshold.

The project does not treat history as a sequence of abstract inventions alone. It asks how technological change enters houses, schools, offices, habits, markets, emotions, and expectations.

### In short

At this stage, the public architecture of the project is:

- **Volume I** — the formation of digital civilization
- **Volume II** — the normalization of networked life
- **Volume III** — the platform organization of ordinary life
- **Now Records** — notes from inside the present transition

This is the current map of the book.

## 中文

**《0.5纪元》** 不是一个松散的文章归档站，而是正在被建造成一部按“卷”组织的人类过渡史：它写的是普通生活如何从大机器时代的尾声，跨入 AI 时代的形成过程。

这个项目同时沿着两条线推进：

1. **回溯性的历史卷**：从大约 1980 年开始，重建过渡年代。
2. **当下记录（Now Records）**：把此刻仍在发生的变化保存为未来的历史材料。

这篇路线图，说明的是前一条主线——也就是历史卷主线——目前已经形成的结构。

### 第一卷——《数字前夜》（1980—1995）

第一卷写的是数字文明成为普通生活之前的形成阶段。

这里有大机器时代的尾声，有早期计算习惯、工具链、办公室系统、培训、可靠性问题，也有网络化思维的逐步进入。到这一卷结束时，互联网已经进入时代中心，但它还没有彻底改写普通生活的组织方式。

一句话说：

> **第一卷写的是数字文明在成为日常生活之前的形成过程。**

### 第二卷——《连接的年代》（1996—2010）

第二卷写的是互联网进入普通生活的年代。

这一卷包括拨号连接、收件箱、搜索、网吧、商业网页、数字记忆、即时通讯、论坛身份、宽带、信息流、民间分类法、触屏、应用、云习惯和日常生活的持续可见化。

一句话说：

> **第二卷写的是互联网如何成为普通生活，然后进一步进入口袋、云端与生活流。**

### 第三卷——《平台时代》（2011—2020）

第三卷写的是平台如何从普通生活内部开始组织普通生活。

这一卷包括移动支付、通讯生态、行为数据、推荐系统、深度学习的公共突破、API 组合、语音界面、隐藏自动化、机器感知，以及大规模远程协作。

一句话说：

> **第三卷写的是平台、移动系统、数据与算法如何成为维持社会连续性的日常机器。**

### 接下来会写什么

下一条最可能出现的历史线，是 2020 年之后的第四卷。

那一卷大概率会写：远程化习惯的加速、基础模型、生成式 AI、代理式工作流，以及平台社会如何进一步转向由机器中介认知与劳动的阶段。

它的最终卷名和精确范围还没有完全确定，但方向已经很清楚了。

### 为什么叫“0.5纪元”

“0.5”这个说法，指的是一种**过渡中的文明形态**。

它意味着：这个世界已经不再完全属于机械文明，但也还没有完全成为 AI 原生文明；人、制度、软件与基础设施都还在穿越一个尚未完成的门槛。

这个项目也因此不只写抽象技术发明，而更关心技术变化如何进入家庭、学校、办公室、习惯、市场、情绪和未来预期。

### 简单说

到目前为止，这个项目的公开结构是：

- **第一卷** —— 数字文明的形成
- **第二卷** —— 网络生活的日常化
- **第三卷** —— 平台对普通生活的组织
- **当下记录** —— 从过渡现场内部留下的实时材料

这就是这本书当前的地图。
