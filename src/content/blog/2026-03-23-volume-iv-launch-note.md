## English

Today, the next boundary of **The 0.5 Chronicles** can be stated more clearly.

The project will **not** rush to define a full fourth historical volume all the way to 2030. Instead, it will first treat **2021–2025** as a more disciplined and historically readable unit.

The title of this next volume line is:

> **Machine Collaborators (2021–2025)**

### Why the boundary stops at 2025

The years after 2020 are too close to the present to be handled carelessly.

2021–2025 already form a meaningful arc: remote-first habits settling into routine, generative AI entering public life, foundation models becoming infrastructure, agent-like workflows beginning to appear, and human-machine collaboration becoming visible inside ordinary work.

That is enough for a historical segment.

But **2026 onward should not yet be treated as settled history**. It is still unfolding. Its patterns are not fully clear. Its meanings are still being negotiated in real time.

### A methodological decision

This is therefore not only a scope decision. It is a method decision.

The project will try to **close earlier periods more carefully**, while treating the still-unfolding present with more patience.

That means:

- earlier years can continue to be organized into formal volumes;
- the near-present should be recorded as living material;
- submissions, observations, workflow notes, and contemporary fragments should be allowed to accumulate before being shaped into later history.

### What this changes structurally

With this decision, the main public historical architecture becomes:

- **Volume I** — Digital Eve (1980–1995)
- **Volume II** — The Age of Connection (1996–2010)
- **Volume III** — The Platform Age (2011–2020)
- **Volume IV** — Machine Collaborators (2021–2025)

And beyond that:

- **Now Records** continues as the place where the present is documented while still alive.
- **Submissions** remains open as a way to gather more voices, scenes, and evidence from the transition itself.

### In one sentence

> **The 0.5 Chronicles will now treat 2021–2025 as the next historical volume boundary, and treat 2026 onward as living present-time material to be recorded carefully rather than concluded too early.**

## 中文

今天，《地球文明0.5编年史》的下一段边界，可以说得更清楚了。

这个项目**不会急着把第四卷一路写死到 2030**，而是先把 **2021—2025** 当成一个更克制、也更适合历史处理的阶段单元。

这条新卷线的卷名先定为：

> **《机器合作者》（2021—2025）**

### 为什么边界先停在 2025

2020 年之后的世界离“现在”太近了，不能草率处理。

但 2021—2025 已经足够形成一段有意义的弧线：远程化习惯沉淀下来，生成式 AI 进入公众生活，基础模型开始变成能力基础设施，agent 式工作流开始出现，人机协作开始进入普通工作的内部。

这已经足以构成一个历史段落。

但是，**2026 年以后不适合被过早当成已定型的历史**。它仍在展开，它的结构还没有完全显形，它的含义也还在现实中被不断协商。

### 这其实是一个方法决定

所以，这不只是一个范围决定，也是一个方法决定。

项目会尝试把**已经过去的阶段更认真地收口**，同时对仍在发生的当下保留更大的耐心。

这意味着：

- 更早的年份可以继续被整理成正式卷；
- 距离现在太近的部分，应先作为“活材料”被记录；
- 投稿、观察、工作流笔记和现场碎片，应该先累积起来，再等待后续历史整理。

### 这会如何改变项目结构

做出这个决定之后，项目公开的历史主结构将变成：

- **第一卷** —— 《数字前夜》（1980—1995）
- **第二卷** —— 《连接的年代》（1996—2010）
- **第三卷** —— 《平台时代》（2011—2020）
- **第四卷** —— 《机器合作者》（2021—2025）

而在这之后：

- **Now Records / 当下记录** 继续承担“记录仍在发生之事”的角色；
- **Submissions / 投稿** 继续作为吸纳更多现场、材料与视角的入口。

### 一句话记录

> **《0.5纪元》从现在起，将把 2021—2025 视为下一段正式历史卷的边界，而把 2026 年以后视为仍需被认真记录、而不宜过早下定论的当下材料。**
